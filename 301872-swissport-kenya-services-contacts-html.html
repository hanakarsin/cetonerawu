Swissport Kenya services and contacts

April 2024 · 7 minute read

Swissport Kenya is the world's leading provider of ground and air cargo services. The company is active in 50 countries, five continents, and 315 airports including Jomo Kenyatta International Airport. This makes it the best-networked ground handling company in the entire universe.

Swissport Kenya contacts are a must have for client companies or people who frequently travel or order cargos by air from different places. Here are various contacts and services that the organisation offers in Kenya.

Services offered by Swissport Kenya

Swissport mainly provides ground handling, cargo, executive aviation and security to its customers in Kenya. They have a vast network of customer airlines, so you don’t have to be worried about the inconvenience. The organisation closely works with Air Arabia, British Airways, EgyptAir, Etihad Airways, Lufthansa, Oman Air, Qatar Airways, Royal Air Force (UK), South African Airways, Swiss International Air Lines, Turkish Airlines, and World Food Programme (WFP).
