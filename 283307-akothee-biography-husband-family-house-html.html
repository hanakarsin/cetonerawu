    Akothee biography

    Akothee education

    Akothee attended high school at Nyabisawa Girls school, but she dropped out in Form 2 when she eloped with her now ex-husband. She later went back to school and in 2004, she sat her Kenya Certificate of Secondary Education (KCSE) at Wasio Mixed Secondary School. She posted her KCSE results slip on social media, and you will be pleasantly surprised to note that this songstress was index number 34, and she scored an aggregate grade of C+. Among the grades that stood out in her result slip is the A plain she scored in History and Government, and the D- that she scored in Mathematics.

    Akothee career

    Akothee’s career has shifted from being a taxi driver, omena vendor, to a now successful musician and businesswoman. She began her taxi driving business when she divorced with her ex-husband. The taxi driving business was based in Kenya’s Mombasa, and she earned approximately Kshs. 3000 to Kshs.4000 daily from the business. Currently, Akothee is a flourishing musician with a couple of hit-songs to her name. She is also a successful entrepreneur, owning Akothee Safaris- a tours and travels company, and an establishment - Morning Star Diani.
