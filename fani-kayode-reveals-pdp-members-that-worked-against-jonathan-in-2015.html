Fani-Kayode Reveals PDP Members That Worked Against Jonathan In 2015

May 2024 · 2 minute read

A former Director of President Goodluck Jonathan’s 2015 campaign team, Femi Fani-Kayode, has rolled out names of prominent politicians who worked against Jonathan’s re-election.

Naija News reports that the former Aviation Minister revealed the identities of some Peoples Democratic Party chieftains that worked against GEJ to install President Muhammadu Buhari.

In a post on his Twitter page on Monday morning, Fani-Kayode asked the named individuals not to keep Nigerians waiting and to publicly confess.

He wondered when former PDP national chairman, Ahmed Mauzu and Jonathan’s former Principal Secretary, Ambassador Hassan Tukur would confess to working against the former president.

He also urged the embattled former National Security Adviser (NSA), Sambo Dasuki, to confess.

Fani-Kayode wrote: “I pray they don’t keep us waiting for too long!

“Now that Babangida Aliyu has publicly confessed to secretly working against Goodluck Jonathan‘s re-election bid in 2015 with a number of others even though they were all in PDP, I wonder when we will hear the confessions of Ahmed Mauzu, Sambo Dasuki & Hassan Tukur as well.”

Recall that former Niger State Governor, Babangida Aliyu, had admitted working against the reaction of Jonathan.

Aliyu while reacting to his suspension by the PDP in Chachanga Local Government Area of Niger, said some governors under the party worked against Jonathan in 2015 because he reneged on an agreement not to seek a second term.

The former chairman of the Northern Governors Forum, said the north would have lost out completely if Jonathan got reelected.

He said after northern governors supported Jonathan to complete the tenure of late President Umaru Musa Yar’Adua, the former president was still allowed to contest in 2011.
