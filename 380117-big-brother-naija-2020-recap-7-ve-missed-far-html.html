Indeed, getting shortlisted among numerous applicants for the Big Brother reality television show is a great deal. However, it is an eviction game, and only one of the housemates is going to win the final cash prize.

So far, various contestants have been evicted for various reasons like having more than three strikes or being voted out. And the exit from the live show through the backdoor, not allowed to give interviews is quite disheartening.

3. Nengi becomes the first finalist

Among the Big Brother Naija 2020 contestants, Nengi has become the luckiest after getting a free ticket to the finale. She became the head of the house after winning the task on September 14.

Nengi scored the highest with 23 points ahead of the other remaining Big Brother Naija contestants. This win meant that she has an automatic ticket to the final week. Also, she is exempted from the next eviction show.

4. Trickytee, Laycon, Dorathy, and Ozo nominated for eviction

Fans will undoubtedly be confused on the next Big Brother Naija 2020 voting session as Trickytee, Laycon, Dorathy, and Ozo are up for possible eviction. This latest turn of events means that Nengi and Neo are already in the finale.

Biggie divided the remaining Big Brother 2020 contestants into two groups that included team white (Laycon, Trickytee, and Vee) and team black (Neo, Dorathy, Ozo). Every team was requested to nominate two housemates for possible eviction.
