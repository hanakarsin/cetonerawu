Former Tahidi High actor Abel Mutua, known to many as Freddy recently left many people green with envy after posting a cute photo of himself with his lovely wife Judy Nyawira.

The actor who doubles up as a scriptwriter on Saturday, March 20, morning shared a photo of himself kissing his wife passionately followed by a very encouraging and appreciation caption.

Freddy, in his caption, noted that he is grateful to his fans for always believing in them and their YouTube show.
