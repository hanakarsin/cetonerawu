Apostle Chinyere Donates Fully Equipped Restaurant To Mr Spellz (Photos)

June 2024 · 1 minute read

The founder of Omega Power Ministry, Apostle Chibuzor Gift Chinyere on Wednesday donated a fully equipped restaurant to Dede Nne, also known as Mr Spells who went viral for wrongly spelling Buhari.

Things got better for Mr Spells after his video went viral, only to return to the trenches after a while.

Mr Spells returned to the streets months after to beg for money to meet his daily needs.

Apostle Chinyere who is known for his donations to Nigerians has decided to take Mr Spells off the street.

The cleric in a post on his Facebook page stated that Mr Spells will no longer beg for food on the street.

According to Apostle Chinyere, Mr Spells’s mother and a salesgirl have been employed to help him run the restaurant.

This is coming weeks after Apostle Chinyere donated a house to the family of Deborah who was killed over alleged blasphemy.

The cleric also sponsored the education of her siblings in school and bought a car for her father’s business.
