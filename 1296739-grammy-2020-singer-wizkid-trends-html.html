Grammy 2020: Singer Wizkid trends on Twitter over Burna Boy's loss

July 2024 · 3 minute read

- Celebrity singer Wizkid is currently a topic of discussion on Twitter

- Music lovers managed to drag the singer into Burna Boy’s loss at the recently concluded Grammy Awards

- Some submitted that a lot of people only wanted the self-acclaimed African giant to win so that they can mock Wizkid

Singer Ayodeji Balogun aka Wizkid is one of the most talked about artists in the Nigerian music industry.

Interestingly, even when the singer is not saying or doing anything on social media, people always have something to say about him.

Following the loss of singer Burna Boy at the recently concluded Grammy Awards, which took place at the Staples Center in Los Angeles, on Sunday, January 26, many have managed to drag Wizkid into the matter.

Some music lovers took to Twitter with the claims that a lot of people only wanted Burna Boy to win the Best World Music Album category, so that they can use the opportunity to drag Wizkid and remind him about how he is far from being the biggest artist in Nigeria.
