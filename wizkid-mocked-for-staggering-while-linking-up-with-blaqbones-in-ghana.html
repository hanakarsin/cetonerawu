Wizkid Mocked For Staggering While Linking Up With Blaqbones In Ghana |Video

June 2024 · 1 minute read

Nigerians on social media have reacted to a recent video of Afrobeat singer, Ayodeji Ibrahim Balogun, better known as Wizkid, staggering uncontrollably after shaking rapper, Emeka Blaqbonez.

Naija News understands that Nigerian rapper, Blaqbonez is currently working on a collaboration with Wizkid, as there is a new video of him recording a new single with Big Wiz in Ghana.

However, the video that got social media reeling with reaction captured the moment Wizkid came out of a room and immediately greeted Blaqbonez with a handshake, which led to the singer losing his balance, whilst staggering to get hold of himself before proceeding into the room again.

Nigerians mocked the singer, as they suggested that he’s probably under the influence of a substance that made him behave that way.
