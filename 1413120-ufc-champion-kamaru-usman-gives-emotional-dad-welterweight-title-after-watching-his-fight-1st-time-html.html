Nigeria's mixed martial artist Kamaru Usman was ruthless during his UFC 261 welterweight title at the VyStar Veterans Memorial Arena in Jacksonville, Florida on Sunday, April 25.

The 33-year-old needed just two rounds to dispatch Jorge Masvidal in their welterweight and maintain his unbeaten status in the sport.

However, the Auchi-born fighter became emotional after the match knowing that his father watched him in action for the first time in 10 years.

Additional information reveals that Muhammed was arrested in 2009 on the charge of multiple false claims submitted by his ambulance company.

Meanwhile, after the match, Kamaru paid tribute to his old man by decorating him with his belt in front of everyone who came to watch the bout and he could not contain his joy in the process.

The picture of the 33-year-old honouring his father quickly circulated through social media with many comments following the post.
