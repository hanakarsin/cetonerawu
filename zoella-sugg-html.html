Zoe Sugg - Age, Family, Bio

March 2024 Â· 1 minute read

YouTuber known as Zoella who, in addition to her videos and blog, has released self-titled UK beauty products and a series of young-adult novels. She has earned over 10 million YouTube subscribers to her Zoella channel and she also has a popular secondary channel titled Zoe Sugg. She became more active on the secondary channel, where she posts vlogs, Q&As, hauls and various makeup and fashion content. 

Before Fame

She created her Zoella YouTube personality as a result of boredom at home in 2009.

Trivia

Her debut novel Girl Online set the record for highest first week sales for a debut author upon its November 2014 release. 

Family Life

Her brother Joe is also a popular YouTuber. Her mother's name is Tracey and her father's name is Graham. She grew up in Wiltshire before moving to Brighton, and she began dating fellow web star Alfie Deyes in 2013. Together the couple is often referred to as Zalfie. In August 2021, they welcomed their first child together, a daughter named Ottilie. In July 2023, she announced she was pregnant with her second child. They got engaged in September 2023. They welcomed their daughter Novie in December 2023. 

Associated With

She has been very close friends with fellow YouTuber Louise Pentland.
