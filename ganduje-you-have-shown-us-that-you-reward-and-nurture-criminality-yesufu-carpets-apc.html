Ganduje: You Have Shown Us That You Reward And Nurture Criminality

April 2024 · 1 minute read

Popular Nigerian activist, Aisha Yesufu has reacted to the appointment of the former Kano State Governor, Abdullahi Ganduje as the chairman of the All Progressives Congress (APC).

Naija News recalls that on Thursday, the National Executive Committee (NEC) of the APC elected Ganduje, as the National Chairman of the party.

He succeeds Senator Abdullahi Adamu, who resigned in the third week of July

Ganduje’s emergence was ratified at the NEC meeting of the ruling party in Abuja.

President Bola Tinubu had shown interest in Ganduje succeeding Adamu.

The National Caucus of the APC had also recommended Ganduje while the immediate past spokesperson for the Senate, Senator Ajibola Basiru was being prepped as the acting National Secretary

Reacting to the appointment, Yesufu opined that Ganduje ought to be incarcerated instead of leading the party.

She insisted that the ruling party has shown that it encourages, nurtures and reward criminality.

Speaking via Twitter, she wrote, “A man that should be facing a incarceration is heading the helm of affairs of APC.

“In what more ways does APC show it is a party that encourages, nurtures and reward criminality?”
