2023: Peter Obi Is Just Being Used, I Know The Person That Lured Him To Labour Party

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Former presidential media aide, Reno Omokri has alleged that the presidential candidate of the Labour Party (LP), Peter Obi is just being used.

Omokri made the allegation in a post on the micro-blogging site, Twitter on Wednesday.

According to him, Obi was lured to Labour Party and is only being used by the person that lured him to the party.

Omokri alleged further that Obi himself is not aware that he is being used but he (Omokri) knows the person pulling the string but he can’t mention his name.

He added that the person is a man that is close to him and he respects so much.

He wrote: “The person who lured Peter Obi to Labour Party is close to me. He is a man I greatly admire. Peter may not know that this man and Tinubu are 5 and 6. I know this man well. I benefited from him. So I can’t mention his name. Even Obi does not know he is being used!”

I’ll Support Only Authentic APC Candidates

The President of Nigeria, Muhammadu Buhari on Wednesday declared that only candidates of the All Progressives Congress (APC) will get his support in the 2023 elections.

The President in a statement by his senior special assistant on media and publicity, Garba Shehu stated that those who defected to other parties or instituted court cases against validly elected APC candidates and the party will not get his support.

He also cautioned those who have defected from the APC to stop linking him with their actions as he remains absolutely committed to the party.

According to Buhari, the APC brought him to power and he won’t do anything that will undermine or jeopardize the chances of the party in the forthcoming election.
